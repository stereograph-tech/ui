Centering

Introduction

This is your .center class. It will expand to take up the available space and center its contents both vertically and horizontally

Example

It's important to note that the height and width of the contents you are centering should be a defined size. You can do that by using the .height{N} or .width{N} classes. 



    <section role="main">
    
        <!-- this is your .center div  -->
        <div class="center">
        
            <!-- this div will be centered. -->
            <div class="width32 height32">
            
                <!-- your contents here -->
            
            </div>
        
        </div>
        
    </section>
