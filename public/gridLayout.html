Example



    <section role="main" class="row">
    
        <div class="col wrap scrollX">
            
            <!-- large cards -->
            
        </div>
    
    </section>

Core Concepts

.col causes the contents of the column to render vertically.

.wrap allows the contents of the column to wrap vertically. Since normally the contents of a column will continue to render vertically even after they overflow the height of the column, wrap causes the contents to wrap up to the top of the column in subsequent inner columns.

.scrollX allows a horizontal scroll bar to appear (if needed), which allows the contents to overflow and stretch the width of the column to contain the large cards.
